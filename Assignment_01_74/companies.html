Nintendo Co Ltd (Nintendo) develops, manufactures and sells electronic home entertainment products. The company develops hardware and software for handheld and home console video game systems, with support from various companies and organizations.

Electronic Arts Inc. develops, markets, publishes, and distributes games, content, and services for game consoles, PCs, mobile phones, and tablets worldwide. It develops and publishes games and services across various genres, such as sports, racing, first-person shooter, action, and role-playing games.

Ubisoft Entertainment SA (Ubisoft) is a provider of gaming services. The company publishes and distributes video games for portable and home consoles, the PC, smartphones and tablets in both physical and online formats.

Activision is a leading worldwide developer, publisher, and distributor of interactive entertainment for various gaming consoles, handheld platforms, and PC, including blockbuster franchises like Call of Duty®, Crash®, and SpyroTM.

The Pokemon Company develops and produces video games, card games, and game software. The Company provides TV animation and films, and manages licensing and quality control business. The Pokemon provides their products and services worldwide.
